Audit Framework

Five-pillar governance model ensuring models are secure, ethical, and legally compliant.

Bias & Fairness

Scanning for proxy variables and demographic markers that cause disparate impact across racial, gender, or age-based groups.

Privacy & PII

Automated detection of PII leakage and verification of data minimization protocols to maintain strict data integrity.

Transparency

Audit of XAI (Explainability). We ensure decision logic is not a black box and can be interpreted clearly by humans.

Social Impact

Assessment of automation risks and potential for workforce displacement or societal polarization in deployment regions.

Governance Hardening Checklist

1

Sanitize Training Sets

Remove PII and correlate variables to ensure no hidden bias proxies exist.

2

Implement Rate Limiting

Mitigate misuse risks by capping requests and filtering adversarial patterns.

3

Document Model Cards

Foster transparency by publishing the model's intended use and limitations.

4

Counterfactual Testing

Verify behavior by testing the model on diverse, simulated demographic cases.
